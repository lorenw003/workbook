FILE: WEEK 1

INSPIRATION: Mosiac Virus, Myriad, Glitch Textiles, Maria Szakats
SKILLS AQUIRED: Basic HTML
This was our first week and introduction to interactive media and WWW. We considered their meaning through how we interact with them - gestures - and their structure or anatomy. We set up VS code and got started exploring HTML.

FILE: WEEK 2

INSPIRATION: Spellverse, Movie Palace Online, 45 Denim Kurata
SKILLS AQUIRED: Paper and Modular Prototyping, Crazy Eights, Githhub
This week we considered how the web has evolved overtime resulting in many different design styles and layouts. We began our own designs starting ideation and learning how to design for the web physically - prototypes - and digitally.

FILE: WEEK 3

INSPIRATION: Parametro studios, Antione Wodniack, Agorespace
SKILLS AQUIRED: Responsive/Fluid design, Media queries, Variable Fonts, Custom Fonts, Audio, Video
This week focused on learning how to publish to the WWW specifically considering grid (layout), responsive design and typography. These considerations informed the personalisation of our workbooks including learning how to use new html tags and css. As well as personal research for our unique visions.
